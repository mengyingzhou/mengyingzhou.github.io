2016 Conference on Online Social Behaviour

Jun 10-11,2016


About / 会议简介

The conference goal is to launch new collaborative research bringing together American and Chinese scholars in big data analyses informed by a deeper understanding of the social science mechanisms by which social networks display and affect behavior.
