James A. Evans

Professor; Director, Knowledge Lab; Faculty Director, Masters Program in Computational Social Science
Department of Sociology
University of Chicago

James Evans’ work explores the sources, structure, dynamics, and consequences of modern knowledge. Evans is particularly interested in the relation of markets to science and knowledge more broadly, and how evolutionary and generative models can inform our understanding of collective representations, experiences, and certainty. He has studied how industry collaboration shapes the ethos, secrecy, and organization of academic science; the web of individuals and institutions that produce innovations; and markets for ideas and their creators. Evans has also examined the impact of the Internet on knowledge in society. His work uses natural language processing, the analysis of social and semantic networks, statistical modeling, and field-based observation and interviews. Evans’ research is funded by the National Science Foundation, the National Institutes of Health, the Mellon and Templeton Foundations, and has been published in Science, PNAS, American Journal of Sociology, American Sociological Review, Annual Review of Sociology, Social Studies of Science, Administrative Science Quarterly, and other journals. His work has been featured in Nature, The Economist, Atlantic Monthly, Wired, NPR, BBC, El País, CNN, and many other outlets.

James Evans 的研究探讨现代知识的来源、体系、动力与重要性。Evans 特别关注市场与科学及知识之间的关系，以及演化与生成模型如何 帮助我们理解集体表征、经验与确定性。他研究了行业合作如何塑造学术科学的规范、保密性与组织；个人与制度网络如何为观念及其创造者 形成创新生态；并考察互联网对社会知识的影响。他的方法包括自然语言处理、社会与语义网络分析、统计建模以及实地观察与访谈。 其研究获得美国国家科学基金会、美国国立卫生研究院、梅隆基金会与坦普尔顿基金会等资助，并发表于 Science、PNAS、American Journal of Sociology、American Sociological Review、Annual Review of Sociology、Social Studies of Science、Administrative Science Quarterly 等期刊；其工作亦被 Nature、The Economist、Atlantic Monthly、Wired、NPR、BBC、El País、CNN 等媒体报道。

Xiaoming Fu

Professor
Institute of Computer Science
University of Göttingen

Prof. Xiaoming Fu received his Ph.D. (2000) in Computer Science from Tsinghua University, then worked with the Telecommunications Networks (TKN) group at Technical University Berlin until joining the University of Göttingen in 2002, where he has been a full professor since 2007. During 2010–2012 he served as Vice Chair of the IEEE Communications Society Technical Committee on Computer Communications (TCCC), and during 2011–2013 as Chair of the Internet Technical Committee of IEEE ComSoc and the Internet Society (ISOC). He is a member of Academia Europaea, an IEEE Senior Member and Distinguished Lecturer, a member of ACM, and a Fellow of IET. He coordinated several EU projects including GreenICN, MobileCloud, CleanSky, and ICN2020, and has won multiple best paper awards.

傅晓明 2000 年获清华大学计算机系统结构专业博士学位；同年任德国柏林工业大学研究员。2002 年起任教德国哥廷根大学，创建计算机网络研究实验室， 先后兼任数学与计算机科学学院学术院长、国际办公室主任等职。33 岁时成为哥廷根大学建校 270 年以来首位华人终身教授。其研究领域包括互联网、 移动计算、云计算、大数据与社会网络；曾任 IEEE ICNP 2013、ACM ICN 2016 总主席等职，并担任多项欧盟科研项目（Green ICN、Mobile Cloud、 Clean Sky、ICN 2020）负责人或首席科学家。

Jar-Der Luo

Professor of Sociology
Chairman, Tsinghua Social Network Research Center
Tsinghua University

Luo Jar-Der is a professor in the Department of Sociology, Tsinghua University, president of the Chinese Network for Social Network Studies, and chairman of the Tsinghua Social Network Research Center. He earned his Ph.D. in Sociology from the State University of New York at Stony Brook. His research spans social capital, trust, social network analysis with big data, self-organization, and Chinese indigenous management research on guanxi and guanxi circles. He organized CNSNS in 2005 to promote network theories and SNA in China and develop theories suited for Chinese management. He also organized the Community Revitalization Research Center at Tsinghua to pilot sustainable revitalization models that integrate public-private partnership, scientific planning, balanced development, step-by-step implementation, and local self-reliance.

罗家德，清华大学社会学系教授、博士生导师，清华大学社会网络研究中心主任，清华大学社会科学学院信义社区营造研究中心主任， 中国社会学会社会网暨社会资本研究专业委员会创会主席。研究方向包括社会网理论、大数据中的社会网分析、自组织过程与中国本土管理 （如“关系”“圈子”）。除学术著作外，团队在灾区乡村及北京大栅栏等地开展社区营造实验，出版《灾区重建纪事》《云村重建纪事》等。
