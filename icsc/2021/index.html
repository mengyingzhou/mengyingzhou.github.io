Welcome

The conference goal is to launch new collaborative research bringing together American and Chinese scholars in big data analyses informed by deeper understanding of the social science mechanisms by which social networks display and affect behavior. The conference is supported by the School of Social Sciences, Tsinghua University and the Center for Social Network Research, Tsinghua University; co-organized by the Tsinghua-Qingdao Institute of Data Science and Tsinghua University Press.
