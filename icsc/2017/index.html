Dates & Venue

Dates: May 30 – June 4, 2017
Venue: Beijing International Convention Center (BICC), Beijing, China

Participant Information

Dear Colleague, welcome to the 2017 Sunbelt XXXVII International Social Network Conference in Beijing, China!

Your Registration Package Includes

  • This printed program
  • A name tag
  • A USB disk (full abstracts are available on the USB)
  • Tickets for the banquet and/or workshops (if you registered for them)

Sunbelt Traditions & Guidelines

  • We strongly recommend that you purchase travel insurance to cover your time in China.
  • Each oral presentation is 20 minutes. Please start and stop at the stated program times, as attendees may move between sessions to catch papers of interest.
  • Session chairs are asked to keep presenters on time.
  • If you are presenting, please load your slides onto the dedicated laptop connected to the projector 10 minutes before the session begins. Using your own laptop will cut into your 20-minute slot.
  • If you use an Apple computer, please ensure your slides are saved in a Windows-compatible format.
  • Lunch breaks are over one hour to give you enough time to enjoy Beijing food.
  • A hospitality suite runs Friday–Saturday, 6:00 PM–9:30 PM in BICC room 303. Please cooperate with our student assistants who will close the suite at 9:30 PM.
  • Please always bring your name tag. Some sessions, such as the keynote and the Freeman Award speech, require participants to show their name tags.

On-Site Payment (if needed)

If you did not complete payment by PayPal or an alternative method, you may pay on site. Please show your invoice or proof of registration. The local organizer will charge the fee according to your registration time: early-bird (before March 10) or regular (March 10 – May 28). Visa and MasterCard are accepted.

Program Updates

The final program has been posted on the 2017 Sunbelt website. Please review it for last-minute changes. Any further updates will be posted at the registration desk.

If you have any questions, please look for the organizers or student assistants — we are happy to help. Thank you for your attendance and contribution. We wish you an intellectually valuable and enjoyable week in Beijing!
