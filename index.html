About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Big Data and Networking (DataNET) Group (opens new window) of the SONIC Laboratory, School of Computer Science at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xin Wang (opens new window) and Prof. Yang Chen (opens new window). In 2023, I visited CPI group at TU Delft supervised by Prof. Aaron Ding (opens new window).

My research interests include Next-Generation Internet Architecture and Protocol, Web Experience Optimization and Communication Energy. I graduated from Lanzhou University in 2019 with a B.S. in Computer Science.
