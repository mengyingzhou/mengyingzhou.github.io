{trust_transfer_www16}Venkatadri G, Goga O, Zhong C, et al. Strengthening weak identities through inter-domain trust transfer, WWW, 2016.

The main idea: This paper proposes a framework using inter-domain trustworthy signals to detect the untrustworthy accounts.

Innovation: Unlike other detection systems, this framework does not need to collect long time activities in one domain because inter-domain trustworthy signals represent the trustworthiness of honest users in other domains.

Problems:

  1. For honest accounts, they are unable to fully participate in the new domain. (such as Bilibili, you are not allowed to send a barrage if you are lower than a typical level)
  2. For domain managers, they need to collect long time activities to detect malicious accounts.

Result:
The evaluation shows that these signals from Twitter or Facebook are indeed useful, especially the age or age difference.

Future:
Identify the untrustworthy content. Nowadays, there are many people on the Internet who carry out rumors or inflammatory posts on different websites. We can control rumors by analyzing the content using inter-domain signal.

{social_bootstrapping_www14}Zhong C, Salehi M, Shah S, et al. Social bootstrapping: how Pinterest and last. fm social communities benefit by borrowing links from facebook[C]//Proceedings of the 23rd international conference on World wide web. ACM, 2014: 305-314.

The main idea: This paper quantifies the extent to which such social bootstrapping is effective in enhancing the social experience of the website.

Innovation:

  1. use a linking bootstrapping model(Link Bootstrapping Samping LBS) which is a variant of the induced subgraph sampling process.
  2. the giant component property is shown in the target graph with LBS.
  3. copying network enriches reciprocity and clustering of the local structure.
  4. As users become more active and influential on the new website, they create proportionally more native links than copied ones. (called ‘weaning process’)

{online_data_social_welfare_www18} Jia Y, Liu X, Xu W. When Online Dating Meets Nash Social Welfare: Achieving Efficiency and Fairness[C]//Proceedings of the 2018 World Wide Web Conference on World Wide Web. International World Wide Web Conferences Steering Committee, 2018: 429-438.

The main idea: use an algorithm to trade-off the efficiency and fairness in online dating meetings.

Innovation: introduce two formations（Online Submodular Welfare Maximization, Nash Social Welfare) to get the best result for efficiency and fairness respectively.

Gong Q, Chen Y, Hu J, et al. Understanding cross-site linking in online social networks[J]. ACM Transactions on the Web (TWEB), 2018, 12(4): 25.

The main idea: survey the cross-site linking in OSN.

Finding:

  1. cross-site linking options
  • distribution analysis for four group of people (TW, FB, TW+FB, Neither) in Gender, Country, Engagement, Register time. ( Fig. 2)
  • behavioral differences among users with different cross-site linking options , such as the number of check-ins, tips, followers, followings, PageRank and percentage of LSCC. (Fig. 3)
  • behavioral differences among users with different registration time , such as the number of check-ins, tips, followers, followings, PageRank and percentage of LSCC. (Fig. 4)
  • consistency analysis of profile
  • a model based on the feature found below to classify whether the enablement in an account.
  1. cross-site information aggregation
  • use cross-site to fix some blank information in twitter to do some survey, such as gender.
  • analyze the consistency of profile in different OSNs
  1. a survey conducted to find the reason why people enable or not enable this function and the degree of consistency of personal information
  • for enabled people: cross-site posting, social bootstrapping, self-explanatory.
  • for unenabled people: privacy, no need, no like, don’t know.

Zhou, Q., Chen, Y., Ma, C., Li, F., Xiao, Y., Wang, X., & Fu, X. Measurement and Analysis of the Reviews in Airbnb.

The main idea: Shed light on understanding the emerging sharing economy from a user-centric perspective.

Innovation:

  1. analyze from three aspects: ①demographic analysis, ②visible interactions between hosts and ③guests, mobility patterns of Airbnb users.
  2. both analyze the static data and dynamic data
  3. use some other data, such as GDP, population to engage
  4. definition home/destination country entropy
  5. review graph (based on the interaction graph), which links along with the time information.
  6. done sentiment analysis to each country if people have even traveled.

Finding:

  1. the review graph is a WCC
  2. verified users or multiple accounts take most part of PageRank.
  3. top 10 graph communities have only or very few dominant countries.

{Kwak10-twitter}Kwak, H., Lee, C., Park, H., & Moon, S. (2010, April). What is Twitter, a social network or a news media?. In Proceedings of the 19th international conference on World wide web (pp. 591-600). ACM.

The main idea: Before this paper, most people think Twitter is a social network. But in this paper, they introduce a new idea: Twitter is a news media instead of a social network.

Innovation:

  1. 在Twitter上的平均跳数是4，远小于六度分离原则。
  2. 依据pagerank、retweet、follower进行排名，retweet和follower类似，但是pagerank不一样。
  3. Twitter和Google Trend不同，和CNN HeadLine相似。
  4. 由于followers和tweets之间的数量在100[Fig.2]之后差距很大，说明并不是单纯的follower去关注消息，不关注的人也会看这些消息。retweet的人远大于follower的人。
  5. Fig14 indicate that many tweets have a very large number of additional recipients due to shorter separation.

Neil, D., Pfeiffer, M., & Liu, S. C. (2016). Phased lstm: Accelerating recurrent network training for long or event-based sequences. In Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (pp. 3882-3890).

The main idea: Introduce an improved cell PLSTM which can deal with more longer or event-based sequences.

Innovation: phased lstm cell

Gong, Q., Chen, Y., He, X., Zhuang, Z., Wang, T., Huang, H., … & Fu, X. (2018). DeepScan: Exploiting Deep Learning for Malicious Account Detection in Location-Based Social Networks. IEEE Communications Magazine, Feature Topic on Mobile Big Data for Urban Analytics, 56(1).

The main idea: structure a model to detect malicious accounts in DianPing.

Innovation:

  1. Cooperate the DeepLeaning(LSTM) with conventional features together.
  2. discuss the percent of two methods in this system.
  3. discuss which feature has the biggest contribution to this system.
  4. add this feature to another excellent model to confirm that this feature is indeed good.

Jin, L., Chen, Y., Wang, T., Hui, P., & Vasilakos, A. V. (2013). Understanding user behavior in online social networks: A survey. IEEE Communications Magazine, 51(9), 144-150.

The main idea: This a review.

Innovation: A comprehensive view about OSNs(online social networks) from

  1. three aspects people: IPSs, OSN service provider, users
  2. four aspects layers: connectivity and interactions(four types of social graph)[user aspect], traffic activity[IPSs], mobile social behavior[service provider], malicious accounts detection[user].
